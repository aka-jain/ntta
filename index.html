Who We Are?

The Noida Table Tennis Academy is founded by a group of currently active and former table tennis players under the guidance of Noida District table tennis association (NDTTA) with a common goal of establishing a premier institute for teaching advance techniques of modern table tennis. The aim of the institute is fairly simple and straight forward as we wish to establish it with a strong will of creating champions and develop respect for the sports culture in the country. We are associted with NOIDA DISTRICT TABLE TENNIS ASSOCIATION & UTTAR PRADESH TABLE TENNIS ASSOCIATION which enables us to be well informed about the latest changes in the rules and regulation of the association. Moreover we have arguably the best team of coaches in North India which will help create the champions of tomorrow.

Our Philosophy.

We strive for excellence in the basic techniques of the sport which are usually underestimated by most, later we start with modern table tennis techniques which will take extra efforts like improving upon physical fitness etc., one of our goals is also to teach the student how to self-evaluate using logic and rationale behind our methods and explain to them how they can further improve. With the right training and commitment, anyone can become a champion.

Every player can be an academic achiever. Everyone, regardless of financial standing, has the right to think, play, and believe in themselves. Facilities

Along with superior coaching techniques the modern era of table tennis demands mechanised and systematic playing environment keeping that in mind we have the following facilities:

  • Synthetic Matting
  • Robot
  • Multiball
  • 1000 DX stag tables
  • Advanced physical exercise
  • Video recording of matches

Since we strive for excellence we pay personal attention on specific problems the students face over-time and focus on the areas that need to be developed and hope to acheive our goals in the desired time period.
